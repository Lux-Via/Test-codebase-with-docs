Our methodology

A 7-phase approach that actually works.

Every implementation follows a structured, proven methodology - built to minimise disruption and maximise adoption.

PHASE 01

Analysis & Planning

Assess current operations, identify pain points, and define system requirements with your team.

PHASE 02

Data & System Setup

Collect business data, establish core infrastructure, and configure the foundational environment.

PHASE 03

Data Import & Configuration

Import your data cleanly and build tailored functional specifications for your workflows.

PHASE 04

Testing & Reporting

Quality assurance across all modules, with custom reports and dashboards developed to spec.

PHASE 05

Training & UAT

Role-based documentation and user acceptance testing so your team is confident from day one.

PHASE 06

Go-Live Support

Hands-on transition assistance as your business adopts the new system - we don't disappear at go-live.

PHASE 07

Ongoing Support

Long-term maintenance and optimisation via service-level agreements as your business evolves.
